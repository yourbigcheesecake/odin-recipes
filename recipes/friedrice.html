Whatever You've Got Fried Rice

Description

This is a no-recipe recipe, a recipe without an ingredients list or steps. It invites you to improvise in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • White or brown rice
  • Diced veggies
  • Meat
  • Couple eggs
  • Lots of chopped garlic and ginger
  • Scallions
  • 3:1 ratio of soy:sesame
  • Gochujang

Steps

  1. To the wok! Cook meat first on high heat
  2. Pour crumbs into a medium bowl; stir in sugar. Add butter, and stir until well combined
  3. Remove meat from pan. Throw in garlic, ginger and scallions
  4. After 30 seconds, throw in vegetables (frozen or fresh)
  5. Make pool for eggies
  6. Throw in sauce, then rice, and mix it all together until steamy
